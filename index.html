AFF Team #34

We are proud to introduce Team #34, a dynamic group of five Georgia Tech students collaborating on an exciting project as part of GT-2340. Our team consists of four Computer Science majors and one Computer Engineering major, bringing together a diverse range of skills in software development, system architecture, and problem-solving.


Our websites are built using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to provide an interactive, user-friendly, and fast experience! We’ve integrated the OpenStreetMap API to offer a detailed and responsive map, highlighting popular restaurants and eateries in the vicinity of Georgia Tech’s Atlanta campus. Whether you’re a student looking for a quick bite or a visitor exploring the area, our map provides an easy way to discover dining options around the campus.
